LTFS-120 Rotary Tube Filling & Sealing Machine

The LTFS-120 is a high-capacity, precise, and robust rotary tube filling and sealing machine. Built for cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, food, and chemical industries, it offers superior filling accuracy and dependable sealing for plastic and laminated tubes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the LTFS-120 work?

The LTFS-120 operates fully automatically. Tubes are loaded into the rotary indexing table, filled precisely to the programmed volume, and sealed using advanced hot-air or ultrasonic sealing technology. The machine then trims, codes, and ejects the finished tube.

What is the price of the LTFS-120?

The LTFS-120 starts at ₹25,00,000 per unit. Pricing varies based on customization options, tube size ranges, and production capacity. Contact us for a precise quote.

What warranty is provided with the LTFS-120?

The machine includes a 1-year comprehensive warranty on mechanical and electrical components. Extended warranties and AMC packages are available for long-term support.

Can the LTFS-120 handle different tube sizes?

Yes. The LTFS-120 supports a wide range of plastic and laminated tube diameters and lengths, featuring quick-change tooling for fast size adjustments to maximize production flexibility.
