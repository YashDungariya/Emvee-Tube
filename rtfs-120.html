Frequently Asked Questions

How does the RTFS-120 Tube Filling & Sealing Machine work?

The RTFS-120 operates with a fully automated rotary indexing system. Tubes are automatically loaded, accurately filled to the programmed volume, and sealed using hot air or ultrasonic technology. It then trims, codes, and ejects the finished tube for packing.
