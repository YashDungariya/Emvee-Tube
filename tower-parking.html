It's the premier choice for luxury hotels, corporate headquarters, and premium residential buildings where space is at a premium and security is paramount.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does a tower parking system work?

A tower parking system is a fully automated system that uses a central lift (elevator) to move cars vertically to different levels in a tower structure. The driver leaves the car in an entry bay, and the robotic system takes over, parking the car in an available slot. To retrieve, the user requests the car, and the system brings it back to the exit bay automatically.

What is the main advantage over puzzle or stacker systems?

The primary advantage is space efficiency and speed for high-density needs. A tower can park dozens of cars on a footprint that would only fit 2-3 cars conventionally. It is fully automated, requiring no user intervention after leaving the car, and offers the fastest retrieval times for a large number of vehicles.

How much ground space is required for a tower parking system?

Amazingly little. A typical tower parking system can store up to 50 cars on a ground footprint of just about 50 square meters (approx. 540 sq. ft.), which is equivalent to only 2-3 surface parking spots.

Is the tower parking system safe for luxury and electric vehicles?

Absolutely. The system uses precise laser sensors to measure vehicle dimensions and ensure it is handled without any physical contact. The smooth, robotic movement is far safer than manual parking. We can also integrate EV charging capabilities, allowing electric vehicles to be charged while parked.
