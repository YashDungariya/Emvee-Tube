Engineered for Ultimate Mechanical Safety

Our mechanical lifts are equipped with multiple, redundant safety systems to ensure the complete protection of your vehicles and operators, without the complexity of hydraulics.

Automatic Multi-Level Locking

Mechanical locks automatically engage at multiple positions in all four posts, providing a secure physical barrier against descent.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the mechanical four-post stacker work?

The mechanical four-post stacker operates using a powerful electric motor that drives a system of heavy-duty chains or steel cables. This system smoothly lifts and lowers the platform. The platform is secured at various heights by automatic mechanical locks, ensuring safety and stability without relying on hydraulic pressure.

Is a mechanical stacker better than a hydraulic one?

Both systems are effective, but mechanical stackers offer distinct advantages. They often provide faster lifting speeds, have fewer potential points of leakage compared to hydraulic systems, and can be more energy-efficient. They are known for their robust performance and reliability in high-usage scenarios.

What are the safety features of this mechanical parking lift?

Safety is paramount. Our four-post mechanical stacker includes multiple safety features: automatic mechanical locks on all four posts, a broken-chain or slack-cable detection system, motor overload protection, limit switches to prevent over-travel, and a prominent emergency stop button.

Can this electric car lift be installed outdoors?

Absolutely. We offer customized outdoor models that feature hot-dip galvanized or powder-coated finishes for superior weather resistance, IP-rated electrical components and motors for safety, and optional roofing structures to protect the vehicles from the elements.
