LTFS-60 Rotary Tube Filling & Sealing Machine

The LTFS-60 is a compact, efficient, and highly reliable rotary tube filling and sealing machine. Designed for cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, food, and chemical products, it delivers precise filling and secure sealing for plastic and laminated tubes.

  • Capacity: 3,600 pcs/hour
  • Power Consumption: 5 kW
  • Material: Stainless Steel (SS304)
  • Voltage: 415 V
  • Frequency: 50 Hz
  • Tube Types: Plastic, Laminated
  • Accuracy: ±1%

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the LTFS-60 work?

The LTFS-60 operates with a fully automated process. Tubes are positioned in the rotary indexing table, precisely filled to the set volume, and sealed using advanced hot-air or ultrasonic technology. The machine then trims, codes, and ejects the finished tube.

What is the price of the LTFS-60?

The LTFS-60 is priced from ₹15,00,000 per unit. The final cost depends on customization, tube size range, and specific production requirements. Contact us for an exact quotation.

What is the warranty on the LTFS-60?

The machine comes with a 1-year comprehensive warranty covering mechanical and electrical parts. Extended warranty and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) are available for enhanced protection.

Can the LTFS-60 handle different tube sizes?

Yes. The LTFS-60 is designed to work with a range of plastic tube diameters and lengths, with quick-change tooling for fast and efficient size adjustments.
