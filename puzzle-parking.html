Puzzle Parking System: The Smart Automated Solution

Emveepark's Puzzle Parking System is a state-of-the-art automated car parking solution designed to maximize parking capacity in tight urban spaces. This semi-automated system allows for independent vehicle access on multiple levels.

Using a combination of vertical and horizontal movement of parking pallets, our system efficiently parks and retrieves cars without needing an attendant. It's the ideal, cost-effective alternative to conventional parking for residential buildings, commercial complexes, and public parking areas across India.

Multi-Layered Safety Framework

Safety is the core of our design. Every puzzle parking system is fortified with redundant safety mechanisms for complete peace of mind.

Photoelectric Sensors

Detects vehicle presence and positioning to prevent operation if a car is not correctly placed on the pallet.

Mechanical Anti-Falling Locks

Dynamic mechanical locks engage to prevent any pallet from falling in the unlikely event of a primary system failure.

Emergency Stop & Alarms

Multiple, easily accessible emergency stop buttons are placed at the control panel to halt all movement instantly.

Limit Switches

High-precision limit switches prevent both vertical and horizontal over-travel, protecting the structure and vehicles.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What exactly is a puzzle parking system?

It's a semi-automated parking system where platforms (pallets) move up, down, and sideways in a puzzle-like manner to store and retrieve cars. The ground level has one empty slot that allows the pallets to shift until the desired car is brought down to the entry/exit level.

How is puzzle parking different from a simple stacker?

The key difference is independent access. In a puzzle system, you can retrieve an upper-level car without moving the car below it. In a basic two-post stacker, the bottom car must be removed to access the top car.

Can the system be installed outdoors?

Yes. We offer fully customized outdoor models with hot-dip galvanized steel structures for maximum corrosion resistance, along with optional roofing to protect vehicles from sun and rain.

What happens during a power outage?

Our systems can be designed with a manual override mechanism or connected to a backup generator (UPS). This ensures that vehicles can still be retrieved safely even during a power failure.
